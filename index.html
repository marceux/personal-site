Social Media

Social Media is a big part of who I am and what I do professionally. Most of my work involves facebook, customer service, and analytics. Community management is truly a passion of mine, and I understand the value of leveraging a community towards a cause. As 22Social's current Community Manager, my goal is to understand and listen to the customer, and then convey their feedback to the right parties to provide better service and a better product experience."

Pro Gaming

Since 2004, I've competed against other gamers in tournament settings. My breakout year for competitions was 2008, playing Super Smash Brothers Brawl, and now, I've dedicated myself to competing and placing well at Super Smash Brothers Melee. Using my experiences as a Pro Gamer, I've written long-form articles and blog posts that relate professional and personal development to my growth as a competitor. I can safely say that I attribute most of my happiness and success to competition and I don't see myself stopping anytime soon.
